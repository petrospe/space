Population Model

This model ensures population stabilizes at sustainable levels based on life support capacity and automation efficiency.

Workforce Optimization Algorithm

Labor Efficiency Function: L_opt = (Q * A) / (ρ * η)

Where: L_opt = Optimal labor, Q = Output target, A = Automation factor, ρ = Productivity rate, η = Efficiency coefficient

This model minimizes human presence while maintaining operational capacity through advanced automation and AI systems.

Interplanetary Waste Management & Recycling Protocol

Earth-Moon Recycling Rule

"Process on Earth what is needed for Earth's restoration; process on the Moon what is needed for space. High-value, low-mass recycling stays on Earth; high-mass, industrial recycling goes to the Moon."

Terrestrial Waste Strategy

  • Legacy waste cleanup (landfills, ocean plastic)
  • Advanced sorting and upcycling facilities
  • Material reuse for lunar infrastructure
  • Zero-landfill municipal waste systems

Lunar Waste Strategy

  • 100% closed-loop life support systems
  • Industrial byproducts as construction materials
  • Cannibalization of all imported components
  • 3D printing with all available materials

Orbital Infrastructure

  • Orbital Sorting Facilities at Lagrange points
  • Microgravity-enabled purity separation
  • Routing based on Earth-Moon Recycling Rule
  • Interplanetary logistics coordination
